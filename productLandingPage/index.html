  • gastroenterology

    Digestion

    The citric acid in lemon juice can help boost gastric acid secretion, which helps break down and digest food. Drinking lemon water before meals may also help promote and improve digestion.

  • nephrology

    Kidney Stones

    Lemon water contains high amounts of citrate, a salt in citric acid that binds to calcium and helps block stone formation. Research suggests that drinking lemon water may help prevent kidney stones.

  • favorite

    Heart Health

    Lemons are a great source of potassium which help keep your heart healthy, build muscle, and aid the body in breaking down and using carbohydrates.

  • microbiology

    Immune System

    Lemons are a great source of vitamin C, citrate, and flavonoids, which can boost your immune system.
